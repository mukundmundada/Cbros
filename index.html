Spearheaded by Abhijeet Chavan and Aniket Chavan (CBROZ), we are a team of experts in various Engineering domains dedicated to provide solutions to varied degree of challenges faced by clients. With extensive experience over a decade now in Research and Development in Electronics, Mechatronics, IoT (Internet of things). Industrial Automation, Consumer Electronics, Special Purpose Machines, we undertake real-life Engineering problems and endeavor to give the best applicable solution, service through a wide array of indigenously-built products and custom solutions tailored to the client requirements.
